I graduated from Imperial College London in July 2022 with first-class honors in Design Engineering - a course that combines engineering expertise, design thinking, and entrepreneurial skills. During my time there I worked as an undergraduate teaching assistant for the first-year maths course, as well as being selected to represent the department at both open days and at the board of examiners meetings. Additionally, I worked at Cambridge GaN Devices for 6 months, leading the development of an automated test suite for next-generation power transformers. During my master's year, I specialised in machine learning, robotics, and IoT which culminated in my thesis looking at the robotic disassembly of waste electronics, using a purely synthetic dataset for training two vision systems and a reinforcement learning agent. I also developed an IoT system for hydroponic urban farming in under-utilised office space.

Outside of work I can often be found toying with a new coding challenge, training in calisthenics, or in the winter, carving down the side of a mountain on my snowboard whenever I get the opportunity.
